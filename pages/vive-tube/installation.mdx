# Installation

Installing ViveTube is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to add it to your Google Sheets environment.

## Installing the Google Sheets Add-on

1.  Open Google Sheets.
2.  Navigate to `Extensions` > `Add-ons` > `Get add-ons`.
3.  In the Google Workspace Marketplace search bar, type "ViveTube".
4.  Find "ViveTube: YouTube Insights" by ViveScript Solutions in the search results.
5.  Click on the ViveTube listing.
6.  Click the "Install" button.
7.  Google will ask for permissions. Review them and click "Continue".
8.  Choose the Google account you want to install ViveTube for.
9.  Review the access permissions again and click "Allow".
10. Once installed, you'll see a confirmation. You can now find ViveTube under the `Extensions` menu in any Google Sheet.
