## 2. Description of Service

ViveReports is a Google Sheets add-on designed to help YouTube content creators, marketers, and analysts track and analyze their YouTube channel performance data directly within Google Sheets. The Add-on fetches data from the YouTube Data API and YouTube Analytics API and populates it into your Google Sheet.

Key features include:

- Fetching channel metadata (subscribers, views, video count).
- Retrieving detailed analytics (views, watch time, subscribers gained/lost, engagement metrics, demographics, estimated revenue for monetized channels).
- Managing a list of tracked YouTube channels.
- Automated data refresh (for paid plans).
- Automated email reports (for paid plans).
- Subscription management with various plan tiers (Free, Trial, Basic, Pro).
