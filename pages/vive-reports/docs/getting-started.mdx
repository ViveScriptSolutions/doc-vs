# Getting Started

This guide will walk you through the first steps to get ViveReports up and running.

## 1. Installation

First, you need to install the ViveReports add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace.

-   **Go to the Marketplace:** [Install ViveReports](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/vivereports_track_video_performance/102 ViveReports)
-   **Click "Install"** and grant the necessary permissions.

For more detailed instructions, see the [Installation](./installation) page.

## 2. Open the Add-on

Once installed, open any Google Sheet.

-   Navigate to `Extensions` > `ViveReports: Track Video Performance` > `Open App`.
-   This will open the main dashboard in the sidebar.

## 3. Connect Your YouTube Account

-   Click the **"Connect New YouTube Account"** button.
-   Follow the prompts to sign in with your Google account and authorize access to your YouTube data.

For more details on this process, see the [Authentication](./authentication) page.

## 4. Add Channels and Refresh Data

-   Once authenticated, you can add the YouTube channels you want to track.
-   Click **"Refresh Data Now"** to pull your first report.

That's it! You are now ready to explore the [features](./features) of ViveReports.
